Introduction

BEFORE YOU START please make sure you have the following:

  • A validated idea (Do you have proof that your idea caters to a need, or a want?)
  • A basic business model (Generally speaking, do you know how to monetize your project?)
  • Market research (Who are your potential customers/clients/users?)
  • A rough sense of costs (It is sadly impossible to launch a start up without investing not only time, but also financial resources. Please dedicate some thought to how much you need and are willing to invest).
  • A clear goal (Whom is the Business Plan intended for? Potential investors, collaborators or is this a roadmap?)
