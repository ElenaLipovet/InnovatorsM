Introduction

This website collects the insights from a project by a group of five students exploring how a master’s thesis can become a startup.

We looked into key topics such as funding opportunities, writing a business plan, and support programs like the HSLU Smart-up initiative. We also interviewed startup founders and attended three startup events to gather practical insights.

The goal of this site is to bring together the knowledge, resources, and experiences we discovered along the way.
