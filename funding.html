Funding Your Startup

A curated list of grants, programs, and key resources to help you secure capital and move forward.

  • Pro Helvetia: Grants for artistic/design projects and prototypes
  • IKEA Foundation: Funding for young designers, sustainable materials, and product development
  • Gebert Rüf Stiftung: Support for innovative science and technology projects
  • Venture Kick: Up to 150k–600k CHF for startup market entry and prototyping
  • Smart-up: Free coaching, coworking, and support for HSLU innovators
  • Careers Service: CV checks, career coaching, job fairs, and mentoring
  • Innosuisse: Innovation mentoring and funding for students/alumni
  • Accelerators (e.g., MassChallenge Switzerland): Equity-free cash and programs
  • Sustainability: Students4Sustainability, Mercator Stiftung
  • App Creation: Talent Kick, BlueLion Accelerator
  • Design/Arts: Berner Design Stiftung, Migros Kulturprozent

Smart-up

This free program motivates students, alumni, and staff to turn ideas into startups through 1:1 coaching (up to 7 hours free), business model workshops, "start-up garage" (coworking spaces), team matchmaking, and events. Integrate your project into your studies (thesis, ECTS modules in entrepreneurship) for synergies. Ideal for your design master's.
https://hub.hslu.ch/smart-up/

Careers Service

Offers CV checks, career coaching, annual job fairs, salary calculator, and personalized mentoring; visibility via job board where companies post opportunities/projects. Connects to 500+ employers/year.
https://careershslu.ch/

Venturelab

National network for students: bootcamps, Venture Briefing (startup funding), challenges, and free training; 1000+ alumni trained. Focus on innovation.
https://www.venturelab.swiss/

Innosuisse

This federal program funds up to 70% of science-based innovation projects (max. 2.5M CHF), with free mentoring via vouchers (1000 CHF for 5h evaluation). Priority access for HSLU student/alumni startups for market entry post-prototype. Integrate it into your HSLU thesis to boost applications. Ideal for sustainable innovations like your Swiss wool master's.
https://www.innosuisse.admin.ch/de

SECO/Federal Aids

KMU portal for business creation: guides on permits (students OK if >18), taxation, insurance; unemployment/alumni access for indirect funding. No direct grants but cantonal loans. Official resources.
https://www.seco-cooperation.admin.ch/en

Students4Sustainability

Inter-university hub (BFH/HSLU eligible): 10k CHF + mentoring for student sustainability projects. Annual call (March deadline). Great for eco-design theses.
https://www.students4sustainability.ch/

IKEA Foundation Switzerland

Swiss foundation grants up to CHF 600k/year for young designers (<32 years): prototypes, product development, exhibitions, scholarships abroad in design/architecture. Supports sustainable material projects like Swiss wool.
ikea-stiftung.ch

Pro Helvetia

Swiss cultural foundation funding artistic projects, residencies, and research trips in design, visual arts, and more. Supports emerging designers with grants and networking. Ideal for HSLU creative theses.
prohelvetia.ch

Berner Design Stiftung

Promotes contemporary Bern-based design via grants and applied arts collection management. Focus on innovative material projects like Swiss wool. Open to students/alumni exhibitions.
bernerdesignstiftung.ch

Migros Kulturprozent

Funds diverse design, visual arts, and cultural initiatives with project grants. Great for interdisciplinary sustainable design ventures.
migros-kulturprozent.ch

Stiftung Mercator Schweiz

Supports culture-education-environment intersections with grants for eco-design projects. Perfect for HSLU sustainability theses.
mercator.ch

SüdKulturFonds

Non-commercial grants for cross-border design/art collaborations (Switzerland + partners). Includes coaching for cultural startups.
suedkultur.ch

BRIDGE (SNF-Innosuisse)

Joint program for proof-of-concept from basic research to innovation. Up to 270k CHF for design-tech hybrids. HSLU-eligible with industry partner.
bridge.ch

Schweizerischer Nationalfonds (SNF)

Grants for scientific design research projects. Funds early-stage innovation with academic focus. Complements Smart-up theses.
snf.ch

Stiftung FHNW

Innovation grants for art/design at applied sciences unis (HSLU compatible). Supports prototyping and market transfer.
stiftungfhnw.ch

MassChallenge Switzerland Accelerator

Equity-free program for early-stage tech startups (incl. apps): up to CHF 1M prizes, corporate partners (Bühler, Givaudan), sustainability tracks. HSLU Smart-up recommended.
masschallenge.org/switzerland

Talent Kick / First Ventures

Up to CHF 150k for UAS students (HSLU) launching spin-offs, incl. app prototypes. Interdisciplinary teams + S2S VC fund (25k pre-seed).
talentkick.ch

ETH Student Project House

Funding, maker space, coaching for ETH/HSLU-linked app ideas. Open ideation to prototype.
sph.ethz.ch

BlueLion Pre-Seed Accelerator

CHF 250k convertible for tech apps: 10-week program on team/fundraising, industry intros. Global founders welcome.
bluelion.ch
