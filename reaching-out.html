Smartup Event: Female Founders

This was the first networking event I attended, and I found it extremely valuable. Three women each gave a short, ten-minute talk, all demonstrating strong confidence and experience in public speaking. Afterwards, we gathered at three tables, where we could either ask them questions directly or simply listen to the discussions.

My take-aways from Celine, Co-founder of PERIOD

  • Start small, dream big.
  • Test your idea early—she began with a single stall at a Christmas market and quickly validated demand while learning about her customers.
  • Invest your own money.
  • Learn to embrace uncertainty.
  • Learners are readers.

Take-aways from Elisa, Co-founder of Yoordl

  • Inspiration can come from anywhere—her idea emerged while waiting for a bill on holiday.
  • Take part in a hackathon.
  • Trust your intuition.
  • Be bold and take action.
  • Build relationships—connect with everyone and add them on LinkedIn; you may need those contacts later.
  • Step outside your comfort zone every day.

Take-aways from Delia, Co-founder of Job-Eagle

  • Entrepreneurs must be all-rounders.
  • Work should feel meaningful, not like a chore.
  • Just do it.
